Metabolism of Cities Living Lab: Strengthening Diversity and Deliberation in Climate Adaptation Planning

List of Team Members and Affiliations:

The Metabolism of Cities Living Lab (MOCLL) working group is composed of faculty members across campus whose research and scholarship centers around sustainability, resilience, and justice for San Diego and Imperial Counties’ local and natural communities.
