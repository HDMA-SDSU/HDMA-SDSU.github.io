7-Day COVID-19 Vaccines & Healthy Places Index (HPI)

This mobile-friendly dashboard created by the HDMA Center compares 7-day vaccine counts and rates to Healthy Places Index (HPI) scores. The Healthy Places Index is a measure of overall neighborhood health determined by comparing a variety of community conditions. Early trends reveal higher vaccination rates in zip codes with higher HPI scores. Explore this disparity using the dashboard's map and "top ten" charts.
