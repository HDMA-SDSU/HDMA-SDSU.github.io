Tracking COVID-19 with Social Media and GIS: The Research Hub of the HDMA Center at SDSU

Through the use of GIS, social media analytics, and data science approaches, the Center for Human Dynamics at the Mobile Age (HDMA) at San Diego State University are developing analytic dashboards, GIS maps, and visualization graphs to track and monitor COVID-19 outbreaks and impacts to our regional and local communities in San Diego and California.
